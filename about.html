About Us

A few words about us

MINGFA CBO is a grassroots social enterprise focused on transforming lives through:

Sustainable aquaculture
Environmental protection
Youth and women empowerment
Gender-based violence prevention
Marine litter management and the blue economy

Our Vision:

A resilient and self-sustaining community thriving through responsible use of natural resources.

Our Mission:

To foster economic growth, social inclusion, and environmental health in Busia County and beyond.

Motto:

Listening to the voice of the people.
