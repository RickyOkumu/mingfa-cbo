Welcome to Mingfa CBO

MINGFA CBO — a community-based organization in Nangina Ward, Busia County, Kenya, dedicated to sustainable development, aquaculture, gender empowerment, and the blue economy. Empowering youth. Conserving our environment. Creating a better future for all.

More

Who We Are:

MINGFA CBO is a grassroots social enterprise focused on transforming lives through:

Sustainable aquaculture
Environmental protection
Youth and women empowerment
Gender-based violence prevention

Our Vision:

A resilient and self-sustaining community thriving through responsible use of natural resources.

Our Mission:

To foster economic growth, social inclusion, and environmental health in Busia County and beyond.

Motto:

Listening to the voice of the people

Our Projects

1. Youth Fish Farming in Busia County

Goal: Backyard ponds, job creation, aquaculture training

Impact: Over 100 youth empowered
2. Financial Literacy & Youth Capacity Building

Goal: Teach savings, investment, and entrepreneurship

Impact: Stronger youth groups and self-reliance
